NSU BLOOD DONATION DRIVE

In our country, each year more than thousands of people rely on receiving donated blood to stay alive. As a result, our blood banks are running out of required blood and highly rely on active and dedicated blood donors, volunteering to maintain a replenishing blood supply to the people in dire need of it. To ensure this adequate supply of blood, North South University Social Services Club assists the collection centers to enrich their capacity and recruit and maintain an active donor pool through our annual campaign “Blood Donation Drive”.

Maharashtra: State council asks blood banks to host donation camps in summer

In order to fulfil the shortage of blood during summer months, the State Blood Transfusion Council (SBTC) has informed blood banks across the state to plan and organise blood donation camps during the summer season.
